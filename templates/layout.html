About Us

We are an Italian restaurant in the heart of Paris aiming to deliver an authentic taste of Italy to the most beautiful city in the world.


Co-owners Lars Jensen, Katia Jaganeau, and Valentin Bonnet all met while studying at the University of Malta on a semester exchange. Brought together by their love of the culinary arts, they decided to bring their favorite cuisine, Italian, to the home country of Valentin and Katia. They pour their very heart and soul into every dish, ensuring that the taste of the food will immediately transport you into the Italian countryside. It's an experience you simply can't miss out on.
